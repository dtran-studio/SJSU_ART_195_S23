Trailer: Tales of the Unknown



Through my artistic vision, I invite viewers into a sneak peek of my animation "Tales of the Unknown". I delve into the imaginative narrative into a world filled with wonder and excitement. I wanted to build a vision of childhood nostalgia where our imaginations never end. I hope that viewers after watching this trailer leave on their own imaginative journeys. Awakening their inner child again and exploring their sense of wonder. Reminding that within all of us is an unknown world within our imaginations.


"Art:Official" DMA SJSU BFA Show, Digital Installation, Hammer Theater 2023
